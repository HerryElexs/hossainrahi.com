Design

My visual style is inspired by studios like Ufotable, MAPPA, and artists like Hayao Miyazaki and Makoto Shinkai. I also blend elements from ZZZ or rban pop-fusion aesthetics, using dynamic compositions, bold colors, and stylized backgrounds that bring energy to my projects.
